Our Company

What's Vector Marketing?

Vector Marketing is the company that sells CUTCO Cutlery. It was founded as Alcas back in 1981 and was changed to Vector in 1985. Nationwide, we have over 250 independently run locations and a headquarters located in Olean, NY.

What's CUTCO?

CUTCO is the world's finest cutlery. CUTCO manufactures cutlery, cookware, flatware, untensils and kitchen gadgets. We are the parent company of KABAR knives, and have an exciting partnership with Utopian Coffee.

How We Do Business

We are a part of the Direct Sales Association, a national association for companies who sell products and services directly to customers. This means we have an exclusive focus on professionalism, integrity, and ethics regarding our practices.
