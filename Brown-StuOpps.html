All American Scholarship

Vector Marketing Winston has developed many All American Scholarship winners. These individuals are the highest sellers in the entire nation. One of our current managers, Tanner Bibey, won the scholarship his first year on the job.

"Being awarded the All American Scholarship truly was amazing. I worked so hard around 18 credit hours at school and 20+ hours of golf practice per week. I really had to take advantage of appointments I set up during my little free time. It gave me a new outlook on my mental tenacity and what I was capable of with the right mindset. What was also great is that I could take what I learned and apply it on the golf course, especially during competitions. "


Tanner is one of the many students who was able to transfer his skills to his true passion.
